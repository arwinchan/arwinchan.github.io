PHOTO ESSAY

Observations through Coach Car Windows

Arwin Chan Fall 2016

For six weeks I went through Canada and the U.S., to see the land and the people that had come to fill it. The overnight trains were the most solitary stretches of the trip—here's some of the things I noticed along the way.
